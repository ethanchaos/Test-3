Meet Ethan Chaos

Developer. Network researcher. Builder of tools that make the internet more accessible and understandable for everyone.

Ethan Chaos is the creator of VPN Config Hub and Chaos Configurations — two platforms dedicated to simplifying complex networking concepts and making them practical for everyday users. His work spans VPN systems, server infrastructure, SNI research, and web development, with a deep focus on how internet restrictions actually work — and how to navigate them.

Whether it's deploying VPS-based VPN infrastructure, building the Chaos SNI Finder tool, or documenting network access realities in restricted regions — Ethan's approach is always grounded in practical experimentation and real-world network behavior.
